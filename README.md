## üîß **Technologies Used**

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React 18, TypeScript
- **UI Components**: Shadcn/UI component library, Tailwind CSS
- **Data Visualization**: Recharts
- **Form Handling**: React Hook Form with Zod validation
- **Blockchain Integration**: Ethereum (Solidity Smart Contracts)
- **Smart Contract Deployment**: **Scroll L2** for faster and cheaper transactions (Layer 2 scaling solution)
- **Database**: **MongoDB** for storing user and organisation data, ensuring fast and scalable data storage.
- **Blockchain Data Indexing**: **The Graph** for efficient querying of blockchain data, such as donor information and leaderboards.
- **Automation**: **Chainlink** for automating smart contract functions, like milestone tracking and fund release, ensuring secure and reliable operations.
- **Open Source API Integration**: **Gemini API** and **Deepseek R1 Model** from Open Router API.

## **üéÆ How It Works for Organizations**

### **1Ô∏è‚É£ Create an Account and Get Verified (KYB)**
Charity organizations can easily sign up on **DeNate**. To ensure legitimacy, each organization must go through a **Know Your Business (KYB)** verification process. Once verified, your organization will be able to create and manage campaigns on the platform.

### **2Ô∏è‚É£ Create a Campaign**
Once verified, organizations can create a new charity campaign. They will set:
- **Fundraising Goals**: Define the total amount of funds needed for the campaign.
- **Milestones**: Break down the fundraising goal into smaller milestones, allowing for easier tracking.
- **Campaign Details**: Add a description, images, and any necessary context about the campaign and its purpose.

### **3Ô∏è‚É£ Denate Will Automatically Set Up Automated Fund Release**
DeNate utilizes **smart contracts** and **Chainlink automation** to ensure funds are released to the charity organization only when specific milestones are met. The process is automated, transparent, and **trustless**‚Äîensuring that funds are handled securely.

### **4Ô∏è‚É£ Track Campaign Progress**
Organizations can **monitor the progress** of their campaigns in real-time. You'll be able to see how much has been donated, which milestones have been met, and track overall fundraising performance.

### **5Ô∏è‚É£ Donor Engagement & Recognition**
Charity organizations can view a **leaderboard** of top donors for each campaign. Engaging with donors and offering them recognition can improve donor retention and participation.

### **6Ô∏è‚É£ Receive Funds**
Once a milestone is reached, the **smart contract** will automatically release the funds to the charity. You‚Äôll receive the funds securely, without delays or human intervention. This automation ensures transparency and trust in the donation process.

### **7Ô∏è‚É£ Ongoing Campaign Management**
Organizations can continue to manage and update campaigns. You can create new campaigns, update milestones, and interact with the DeNate community. Additionally, you can **deactivate** a campaign at any time, and once deactivated, the **remaining funds will be released immediately** to the charity organization. Your **Donor Dashboard** will show all ongoing campaigns, their statuses, and a list of contributions.

### **8Ô∏è‚É£ AI-Driven Donation Forecasting**
To assist with campaign planning and decision-making, DeNate provides an **AI-driven donation forecast** chart. This feature uses AI to predict the potential donation growth and estimate when your campaign is likely to reach its funding goal. The chart helps organizations to:
- **Visualize donation trends** over time.
- **Predict future donation milestones** based on historical data.
- **Plan better fundraising strategies** by understanding donation patterns.
